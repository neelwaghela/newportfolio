Infos
Thibaud is a swiss cinematographer whose creativity is deeply influenced by the mountains. He thrives in adventurous filming conditions and studio environments, collaborating with teams to create unique worlds. His goal is to capture the beauty and essence of stories through camera and lighting.
Vision
My aim is to make the camera an integral part of the story, using light, framing, and movement to create a narrative presence. I focus on the human element, seeking to convey authentic emotions and establish enriching collaboration with my teams. My ambition is to create immersive and touching cinematic experiences that resonate deeply with the audience.
